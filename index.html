Hello from the Darkness!

24/04/2020 23:50

This is the first time I've posted something online and am kinda nervous how this would pan out. Anyway, I created this blog mainly to save myself from the pathetic loneliness of my life but it con be a useful tool for distraction in this very tough time. This blog doesn’t really have a theme or a topic. This is just a abstract collection of my thoughts, diary entries from my teenage years, personal recollections, pop culture views, etc. I hope that I dont fuck this up like everything else in my life. I really had fun making this and hopefully you’ll enjoy this too. If you have any queries or suggestions, feel free to leave a message.

-Shravan


P.S: I know that this is a tough time and it is of utmost importance to maintain both your physical and mental health. If you are struggling with your mental health, please reach out to people you know and trust. A lot of other bloggers and I are ready to help you, so please feel free to DM us. We are all here to help you.
